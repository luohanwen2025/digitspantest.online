Science & Research

Backed by decades of cognitive psychology and neuroscience research

Psychological Foundation

The digit span test was developed by cognitive psychologists to measure working memory capacity, one of the most important components of human intelligence and cognitive function.

Neuroscience Insights

Neuroimaging studies show that digit span tasks activate the prefrontal cortex, parietal cortex, and other brain regions crucial for executive function and attention.

Research Applications

Used in clinical psychology, educational assessment, and cognitive neuroscience research to evaluate memory deficits, track cognitive development, and study brain-behavior relationships.

Validated Assessment

Digit span tests are standardized, validated assessment tools with established reliability and validity metrics in psychological research and clinical practice.
